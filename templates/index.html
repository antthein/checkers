  • Movement: Move pieces forward diagonally on dark squares.
  • Captures: Jump over opponent pieces to capture them. Captures are mandatory!
  • Multi-Jumps: If you can jump again after landing, you must continue jumping.
  • Kings: Reach the opposite side to become a King. Kings can move and capture backwards.
